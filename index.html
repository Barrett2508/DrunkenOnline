Welcome to Drunken Lullabies!

We are a full PvE cluster for ARK Survival Evolved on PlayStation. We have been active since 25th February 2021 and have been growing ever since! We started this cluster to give players a safe and enjoyable environment to enjoy the game and this continues to be our mission until this very day!

We have an excellent admin team who are happy to help you out with any in-game or discord issues that you may have, as well as a friendly community who are also happy to help you out where they can!

We hope you enjoy your time with us on our cluster, so go on out there and have fun!
